##  How R Started

R was created in the early 1990s by two statisticians:

- [**Ross Ihaka**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ross_Ihaka)
- [**Robert Gentleman**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Gentleman_(statistician))

They were working at the **University of Auckland (New Zealand)**.

**Goal:**  
To create a free and powerful tool for statistical analysis, because existing software at that time was expensive and limited.
