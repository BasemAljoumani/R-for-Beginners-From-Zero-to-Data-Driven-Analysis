
# Course Announcement

## R for Beginners: From Zero to Data-Driven Analysis
*A solidarity-based course supporting Sudanese students and researchers*

This course is offered as a solidarity-based educational initiative to support Sudanese students and early-career researchers who are currently facing significant barriers to accessing higher education due to the ongoing war in Sudan.

The initiative is led by **Dr. Basem Aljoumani** (Technische Universität Berlin) in collaboration with **Assoc. Prof. Sarra Saad** (Guest, Technische Universität Berlin).

The course aims to equip participants with practical and transferable skills in **R programming for data analysis**, with a particular focus on environmental applications such as **water and soil data**.

## Registration

- Selected participants will be notified by email
